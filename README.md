### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **backend-focused engineer with 6+ years** building production systems that hold up under real load. I take ambiguous product problems and turn them into reliable, well-tested services and APIs.

- 🔭 Currently engineering at **[@fielded](https://github.com/fielded)** — a healthcare supply-chain platform serving public-health programs across Nigeria, Rwanda and other African markets.
- ⚙️ My core is **Node.js, NestJS and Express**, backed by **PostgreSQL, MongoDB, CouchDB and Redis**, with async/event-driven work on **SQS, SNS, EventBridge, RabbitMQ and Bull**.
- 🏗️ I've architected entire backends from scratch — domain modelling, order-execution engines with idempotent state transitions, payment & subscription integrations (Paystack, Apple App Store), and CI/CD to production.
- 🧪 I ship with **strong test coverage** (unit + integration + e2e) and care about concurrency-safety, data integrity, and clean domain boundaries.
- 🌱 Actively growing my **Go** proficiency; comfortable on the frontend with **React + TypeScript** when a feature needs owning end-to-end.

Built the order-execution engine (market-hours gating, idempotent buy/sell/short lifecycle, P&L), an Apple App Store subscription system, and Paystack payment integration with webhook verification and idempotent reconciliation.
